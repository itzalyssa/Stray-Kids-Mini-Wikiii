Stray Kids (스트레이 키즈 seuteurei kijeu) or can be abbreviated to SKZ, is a South Korean boy band under JYP Entertainment. The band was formed in 2017 through a male idol debut project - Stray Kids survival reality show in which the 9 starting members will debut as a team. Orinally debutting with 9 on March 25, 2018, in 2019, one of the members left for personal circumstances resulting in the group to continue with only the 8 remaining members. Current members are Bangchan, Felix, Hyunjin, Han, Lee Know, Changbin, I.N, Seungmin and former member is Woojin.
