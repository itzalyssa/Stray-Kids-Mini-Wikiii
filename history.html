HISTORY

2017

Stray Kids was formed by JYP Entertainment through a reality show called the Stray Kid's Survival Show. This 2017 reality show was created by JYP Entertainment and MnetJYP Entertainment and Mnet. Participants are, Bang Chan, Felix, Lee Know, I.N, Seungmin, Han, Hyunjin, Changbin and Kim Woojin. The show resulted in the elmination of Lee Minho (Lee Know) because of his poor confidence in dance and his rapping skills. Felix was also elimiated, reason wise, because of his lack of Korean language skills. Both eliminated members returned on the last episode for a second chance in which they both made it back into the group. The show aired on from October 17 to December 19, 2017 in Korea on Tuesdays at 23:00 for 10 episodes. The group deputed with all 9 trainees.

2018

Pre-dubut, they released their extended play Mixtape in January and officially debuted on March 25, 2018, with the release of their extended play, "I Am NOT" on March 26 followed by 2, "I Am" series, "I Am Who" and "I Am You". The groups first overseas performace since their deput was at KCON JAPAN 2018 on April 14. They released a comeback album, I am WHO On August 30. Stray Kids won ther first award at the Soribaba Best K-Music Awards called "The Rookie Award".

2019

January 2019, 2019, Stary Kids went on their first touring perfomances for their UNVIEL tour. Their forth mini album, Miroh was realeased on March 24, 2019. Over 100,000 albums were sold in the first month. On October 28, JYPE annouced that Woojin had left the group due to personal circumstances leaving Stray Kids to continue with 8 members.

2020

Their world tour, "District 9: Unlock" ended early due to the COVID-19 pandemic. On May 26, SKZ annouced their comeback with their first studio album, "GO LIVE". The album was released on June 17 and sold over 200,000 units in the first month. This was a major milestone as it was the start setting foundation for the "Stray Kids genre" developed by the group over the years. They had an online concert on November 22 to make up for the cancellation of their World Tour.

2021

They held their first fan meeting "SKZ-X" on February 20, 2021. Days later, accusations online against Hyunjin claimed of him being a school bully. This led to Hyunjin going on a hiatus and publishing an apology letter to the groups instagram. At the commpetition show, "Kingdom: Legendary War," the group appeared as 7 members to participate in the event. On June 3, it was annouced that even with 1 less member, they had won the show. Stray Kids released their second studio album "NOEASY" on July 22 and became a milestone for the group. It was both Stray Kid's and JYP Entertainments first million seller album, with 1,127,800 copies sold the first month. They also composed a holiday special single, Christmas Evel and Winter Falls as a gift for fans.

2022

They had their second fanmeeting and 2nd World tour. They held their second anf first offline fanmeeting, SKZ CHOCOLATE FACTORY on February 12 and 13, 2022. ODDINARY album was released March 18, marked Stray Kid's first release to debut at No.1 on Billboard 200 and their second million selling albim with 1,546,907 copies sold within the first month. Second World Tour, "Maniac" on April 29, 2022 which was their first offline concert in 2 years since the pandemic. MAXIDENT album was released on October 7 and became Stray Kids first million seller on the day of release and the second album in a row to hit on Billboard 200.

2023

"Maniac" world tour completed on April 2. On June 2, Stray Kids released their third studio album "5-Star" which themes with the group's fifth anniversary. The release of the album once again, Stray Kids achieved being the most pre - ordered album in K-pop history and concesutivly continued to debut at No.1 in Billbaord 200. They attended their first MTV Video Music Awards - their first American award show and recieved "Best K-Pop." Stray Kids became Asia embassadors for Tommy Hilfiger in September of 2023. In November 11, 2023, they released their album "ROCK-STAR" which landed on the No.1 on Billboard 200 for another year. Their album title track, "LALALALA" was the first entry of the members careers on the Billboard Hot 100 at No.90. Their album sold over 4 million in the first month.

2024

On May 2024, Stray Kids made history as the first and only K-pop group to attend the Met Gala. They were invited by Tommy Hilfiger and wore outfits from the brand. They annouced their third world tour, "dominATE" spanning from 2024 - 2025 covering major cities across Asia, Europe, North America and more. The tour supports their new mini-album, "ATE" which was released in July 2024. On July 23, 2024, SKZ releasedthe song "SLASH" as part of the OST for the 2024 film, "Deadpool and Wolverine".
